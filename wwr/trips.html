Our half-day whitewater trip last 4 hours and includes lunch. It is led by a professional rafting guide. Recommended for children and adults, ages 5 and up. Good for beginners wanting to try out the sport.

Our full-day whitewater trip is 8 hours and includes lunch. It is led by a professional rafting guide. Recommended for children and adults, ages 8 and up. This is a great day activity for the whole family!

Our multi-day whitewater trip lasts three days. Meals are provided. This trip is led by a professional rafting guide. Recommended for children and adults, ages 10 and up. This is for people that have some experience whitewater rafting.
